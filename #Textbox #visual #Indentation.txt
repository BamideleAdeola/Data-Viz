#Textbox #visual #Indentation

Have you wondered how you can indent (Increase or decrease) your text visual in Power BI? 

With February update, this is now possible.

You can also use the Tab or Shift+Tab keys to accomplish the same.

Tab 		- Increase indent
Shift+Tab 	- Decrease indent
