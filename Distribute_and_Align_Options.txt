#Distribute and #align option in #Power BI

Alignment in Power BI refers to the placement and arrangement of visual components inside a report or dashboard. 
It contributes to the organization and aesthetic attractiveness of the visualizations and other components.

The distribute and align options allow you to select multiple visualizations or objects by holding the Shift key and clicking on each item. 
Click on format tab and choose the "Align" or "Distribute" option from the context menu.

The Align option allows you to align the selected objects horizontally or vertically, ensuring they are evenly spaced.
The Distribute option helps you distribute the selected objects evenly along a horizontal or vertical axis.
